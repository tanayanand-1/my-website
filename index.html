COMPANY PROFILE

+

Tanayanand Pvt. Ltd. is a company specializing in the design, development and manufacturing of electric ground support equipment (GSE) for the aviation industry. With a focus on sustainability, efficiency, and safety, we provide innovative electric equipment to support aircraft operations on the ground. Our products are engineered to meet the growing demand for environmentally friendly and cost effective ground support equipment.

OUR VISION

+

Our vision is to lead the industry into a sustainable and eco-conscious era. We are committed to pioneering innovative electric equipment that redefines the way modern world function. Our goal is to create a world where efficiency, environmental responsibility, and cutting-edge technology coexist harmoniously, setting new standards and inspiring a cleaner, brighter future.

OUR MISSION

+

We prioritize safety, operator comfort, and reduced operational costs while ensuring ease of operation and versatility in our equipment. Our unwavering commitment to environmental stewardship drives us to create solutions that are not only efficient but also environmentally friendly. Through innovation, integrity, and dedication, we aim to inspire positive change and lead the way towards a sustainable future for generations to come.
